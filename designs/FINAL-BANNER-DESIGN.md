## Why This Design Wins

1. **Shows real product** - Actual tombstone cards with epitaphs
2. **Darkly memorable** - "13 Broken Promises" sticks in your head
3. **Demonstrates AI** - The epitaphs show AI capability
4. **Emotional hook** - "Why Your Tasks Die" is relatable
5. **Professional** - Looks like a premium SaaS product
6. **Unique** - No other meeting tool has a graveyard
